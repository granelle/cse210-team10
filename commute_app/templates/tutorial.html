Find out how good your home's location is.





Searching for a new place to live? Location would be a crucial factor. Everyone has different standards of what a good location is — you may want somewhere that is easy to drive from to your everyday work, or you may want somewhere that is walkable, bikable, or has access to public transportation. Perhaps you don't care much about commute, and you perfer a place that has better access to resturants or groceries. Our website helps you get personalized scores of your potential new home based on your location preferences.
